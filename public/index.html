Instructions:

Movement:

Press and hold the space key to move and point your weapon towards the mouse pointer.
Try to kill your opponent using your weapon.
Press M to mute and unmute the volume.

Health:


This is your health bar. The more it goes down, the more at risk you are of dying when you get hit next*.
*Because of Boomba Zoomba's locational damage system, your health bar may not be at zero when you die. But if its in the red zone, you should be very careful.
